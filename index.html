❓ Help & How It Works
🚀 What is Zidea?

Zidea is your all-in-one platform to generate, save, and share ideas — powered by AI. Connect with friends, challenge them to games, and build your idea library.

💡 How to Generate Ideas
  1. Go to Generate Ideas in the menu
  2. Pick a category, difficulty, and audience
  3. Optionally describe your topic
  4. Hit ✨ Generate Ideas
  5. Save or star ideas you like
📚 Library & Favourites

Save ideas to your Library. Star your favourites. Tag them to stay organised. Access everything from the Library tab.

🔥 Explore

Browse trending ideas from the community. Filter by category. Get inspired by what others are building.

💬 Chat & Arcade

Message friends directly, make voice calls, and challenge them to Chess, Among Us, Tic-Tac-Toe and more in the Arcade.

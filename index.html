❓ Help & How It Works
🚀 What is Zidea?

Zidea is your all-in-one platform to generate, save, and share ideas — powered by AI. Connect with friends, challenge them to games, and build your idea library.

💡 How to Generate Ideas
  1. Go to Generate Ideas
  2. Pick category, difficulty, budget, time
  3. Hit ✨ Generate Ideas
  4. Save, star, improve, or share ideas
💬 Chat & Calls

Message friends directly, make voice calls, send voice messages and files. Create groups for group chats and group calls.

🎮 Arcade

Play Chess (5 difficulty levels), Tic-Tac-Toe, Among Us, and Rock Paper Scissors — vs AI or friends.
